Deeper

Summary

Deeper, a 2D action-survival-adventure game where players are tasked with drilling into a dangerous dig site in search of a valuable diamond. Many dangers lurk below -- extraterrestrial life seems to have invaded underground. Drill strategically, buy items and upgrades, and don't run out of light. Discover what awaits when you go deeper.
